 Pre-Flop 


The PreFlop is the first stage of poker, this is where the players are all dealt 2 'hidden' cards. These cards are kept hidden to all players except those to who they belong to. After being dealt these cards, 5 cards are added face up to the table (in the order shown below). A player makes their hand.
