The Game of Poker

Poker is a turn based game. The are stages of play come in an order: pre-flop, flop, turn and then the river. Players make betting actions during these phases. Players can fold, bet, raise or check.

Fold

A fold means the player forfeits the round, places their cards out of their hand and sits out.

Bet

A bet means the player is placing a bet into the community pot of chips.

Raise

A raise means the player is increasing the minimum bet size with their own bet.

Check

A check is when the player does not bet further as they have already put in an amount that satisfies the current bet.



 Pre-Flop 


The PreFlop is the first stage of poker, this is where the players are all dealt 2 'hidden' cards. These cards are kept hidden to all players except those to who they belong to.
